Help

To book a hospital bed, log in to your EverHeal account, navigate to the "Booking Beds" section, select your preferred hospital and bed type, and confirm your booking with payment options available.

Use the "Find Doctors" feature to filter by specialty, location, ratings, and availability. You can also read patient reviews and book consultations directly through EverHeal.

Go to "Upload Health Records" in your profile, choose the files from your device, and upload them securely. Supported formats include PDF, JPG, and PNG.

Yes, EverHeal offers online consultations. Select "Online Consultation," choose your doctor, pick a time slot, and complete the booking with payment.

In emergencies, immediately call your local emergency number. You can also use EverHeal's "Emergency Help" feature to find the nearest hospital or request urgent assistance.

Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your registered email, and follow the instructions sent to your email to reset your password securely.
